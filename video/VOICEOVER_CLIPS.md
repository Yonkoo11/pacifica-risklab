# RiskLab Demo — Voiceover Clips

Framework: PAS (Problem → Agitation → Solution)
Target: 70-80 seconds
Audience: Pacifica hackathon judges (Analytics & Data track)

---

## Clip 01 — Hook (Result First)
**Frame:** Simulation results showing 3.9/10 CRITICAL, 612 liquidations
**Audio:** "Survival score: 3.9 out of 10. Critical. 612 positions liquidated in a single crash."
**Gate:** Opens with the most dramatic output. No intro, no logo, no context. Judges see the danger immediately.

## Clip 02 — Problem
**Frame:** Pacifica market selector showing 63 markets, BTC 50x
**Audio:** "Pacifica offers up to 50x leverage across 63 markets. But nobody's tested what happens to these parameters during a real crash."
**Gate:** Names the specific problem. Uses Pacifica's own numbers.

## Clip 03 — Agitation
**Frame:** Scenario selector with EXTREME/CATASTROPHIC badges
**Audio:** "Gauntlet charges one point six million dollars a year for this kind of stress testing. There's no open source alternative."
**Gate:** Makes the gap concrete with a dollar amount. Judges understand the value instantly.

## Clip 04 — Solution walkthrough
**Frame:** Full app with controls on left, cascade chart on right
**Audio:** "RiskLab pulls live parameters from Pacifica's API. Pick a market. Pick a historical crash. Set your hypothetical open interest. Hit run."
**Gate:** Shows the system working. Four short steps.

## Clip 05 — The hard part
**Frame:** Cascade chart zoomed — gradient bars accelerating, price line dropping
**Audio:** "The engine generates a thousand synthetic positions, then replays the crash minute by minute. Each liquidation hits the price. The price drop triggers more liquidations. That's the cascade."
**Gate:** Technical depth. Shows the non-trivial simulation logic.

## Clip 06 — Compare mode
**Frame:** Compare mode showing CRITICAL vs STABLE, diff section with green numbers
**Audio:** "Compare mode shows the impact of changing parameters. Drop leverage from 50x to 20x. 224 fewer liquidations. Survival jumps from critical to stable."
**Gate:** The "wow" moment. Parameter change = measurable safety improvement.

## Clip 07 — Close
**Frame:** Full app, clean shot of RiskLab header + results
**Audio:** "RiskLab. Self-serve parameter stress testing. Built on Pacifica."
**Gate:** Product name, one-liner, chain attribution. Hold 4 seconds.
